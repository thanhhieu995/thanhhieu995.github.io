Privacy Policy for Money Manager

Last updated: June 2026

Money Manager ("the Application", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and disclosed when you use the Money Manager application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

If you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive:

  • Email address
  • Google Account identifier
  • Display name
  • Profile picture (if provided by Google)

This information is used solely to authenticate your account and enable cloud synchronization features.

Financial Data

Money Manager allows you to create and manage personal financial records, including:

  • Transactions
  • Accounts
  • Categories
  • Budgets
  • Bookmarks
  • Application preferences

This data is created and controlled by you.

Usage and Diagnostic Data

We may collect limited technical information necessary to maintain and improve the application, including:

  • Device information
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Error reports
  • Performance diagnostics

2. Cloud Synchronization

Money Manager provides optional cloud synchronization.

When you sign in and enable synchronization, your financial data may be securely stored in cloud infrastructure to synchronize information across your devices.

Cloud-synchronized data may include:

  • Transactions
  • Accounts
  • Categories
  • Bookmarks
  • Synchronization metadata
  • Application settings related to synchronization

Cloud synchronization is optional. You may continue using the application locally without enabling synchronization.

3. Premium Subscription

Money Manager offers optional Premium features through subscription purchases.

Premium purchases are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing.

We do not collect, store, process, or have access to:

  • Credit card numbers
  • Bank account information
  • Payment credentials
  • Billing passwords

We may process subscription status information solely to determine whether Premium features should be enabled on your account.

4. Advertising

Money Manager may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may automatically collect information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Approximate location information
  • Application usage information

This information may be used by Google for:

  • Ad delivery
  • Ad measurement
  • Fraud prevention
  • Ad personalization (where permitted)

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Services

The Application may use third-party services including:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

6. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide application functionality
  • Enable cloud synchronization
  • Authenticate users
  • Restore user data across devices
  • Verify Premium subscriptions
  • Improve application reliability and performance
  • Provide customer support

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

Cloud-synchronized data remains associated with your account until:

  • You delete the data
  • You request deletion
  • Your account data is removed according to our retention policies

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using industry-standard security practices.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your information
  • Modify your information
  • Delete local application data
  • Request deletion of cloud-stored data
  • Stop using synchronization features
  • Uninstall the application at any time

10. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence.

By using the Application, you consent to such processing where permitted by applicable law.
